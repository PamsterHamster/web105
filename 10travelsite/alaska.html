If you love the outdoors and/or appreciate beautiful scenery, then Alaska is the place for you. Surrounded by majestic mountains, glaciers, waterways forests, hiking trails and vast spaces of open land, there is something for everyone here, of all abilities, year-round.

For the rugged outdoorsman, the nature here will challenge even the fittest of men and awe you with wonder. For those who prefer the more passive approach to appreciate natural beauty, some activities are adaptable to a less rigorous pace. Come explore the state via cruise ship, or reach other destinations by air and some by vehicle. Alaska welcomes you.
