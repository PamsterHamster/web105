I picked up the viola at age 10. My friends and I began a string quartet (shown here-I was 13yo), rehearsing 3 hours every weekend.
As teens, we performed gigs all over Chicagoland, mostly weddings/receptions and corporate parties.
It was my first real job and taught me the importance of dedication, patience, commitment and entrepreneurial skils.
